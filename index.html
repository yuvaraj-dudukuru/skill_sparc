Hill Stations

Darjeeling (West Bengal): Known for its tea estates, panoramic views of Mount Kanchenjunga, and colonial architecture. Gulmarg (Jammu and Kashmir): A popular skiing and trekking destination, known for its stunning landscapes. Srinagar (Jammu and Kashmir): Famous for its Dal Lake, Mughal Gardens, and houseboat stays. Idukki (Kerala): Offers scenic views, the Idukki Arch Dam, and wildlife sanctuaries. Leh (Ladakh): The highest hill station in India, known for its unique culture and high-altitude landscapes. Mount Abu (Rajasthan): The only hill station in Rajasthan, known for its lakes, temples, and stunning views of the Aravalli Range. Kasol (Himachal Pradesh): A popular destination for backpackers, known for its unique atmosphere and hippie culture.
